CODE BLACK

"The Code Was Stolen. The Truth Was Hidden. The Hunt Begins"

Code Black follows Janlie, an undercover industrial spy, who steals a crucial blueprint from a high-tech company. However, his mission takes a dangerous turn when he's caught by Jayzon, a security guard with a secret agenda. After a series of escapes and betrayals, Janlie uncovers a sinister plot by his boss, Mark Louil, who is creating a global surveillance system to control the world's devices.


Key Characters:

Janlie - The protagonist and skilled spy working for a rival company.

Jayzon - A seemingly simple security guard who secretly investigates and plays a pivotal role in Janlie's pursuit

Jeans Smitch Enopia - Janlie's teammate, later revealed to have a hidden agenda.

Justin Gaviola - Another of Janlie's teammates, caught in a web of betrayal.

Kimberly Silvano - A tech expert and hacker, helping Janlie out of tight situations.

Mark Louil - The boss and mastermind behind a global surveillance conspiracy, with a dark secret.


Genre:

The film is a thriller with elements of action and suspense, exploring themes of industrial espionage, betrayal, and global surveillance. It keeps viewers on edge with its fast-paced plot and unexpected twists.


Directed By:

Ma. Jasce Nova Belia
